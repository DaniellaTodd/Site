---Sacredly Profane Muffling---

‘Spirits possess and ride anybody.’ Stallings

Nonhuman entities make depository announcements on all surfaces in erotic spaces, till every interface swells into a singular membrane. The body is undone as territory and a less timid information system replaces all iterations of singularity. This augmenting relation forgoes the appearance of elegance (concealment) for the blabbing froth of collective emittance.

Desires of no end or beginning snatch various forms depending on the available materials - sweat, wood, fingers, waist, glass, shadow, tap, mouth, bog, shoe, whisper. These desires are spirits with multiple and infinite authors, defying mortality through access and excess. They mediate every interplay, participating and collaborating on all ecstatic potentials.

‘Sex is so weighed down by human ethics that there is little to no simultaneous valuing of sex as art, performance, collaborative interaction between humans and nonhumans, and as a nonhuman force acting and being aesthetics beyond sight’ Stallings

Our own incomplete and over-exalted senses barely function within the ‘provable’ infrastructures of ‘reality’, imagination is one tentacle worth stretching out from the viscous orbit of (taught and rehearsed) tales that place us at the centre. Ecstasy troubles centrality, and dissolves hope that we can keep ourselves together. The filling and spilling of oneself/itself/ourselves is also a process of splintering, with perforations felt through sharp exhales, the fuzz of no thoughts, a room caressing and entering you.

‘On the physical side it’s dancing without self consciousness that one is dancing. Being danced. Opening the body, turning it over to the DJ.’ Mckenzie Wark, twitter
